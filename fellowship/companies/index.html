HackDFW Fellowship

Join us in making Dallas the place to be for the top student developers.

Join the Program

Information for Companies

The HackDFW Fellowship is Dallas's premier internship program, run by the team that produces HackDFW. As the largest hackathon in Texas, we offer unparalleled access to the best student developers in Texas, partnered with some of the best names in Texas technology. We started the Fellowship program to give back to the companies and community that supports us by bringing in some of these top students to Dallas, to encourage them to come to Dallas and make their mark in the community here.

Timeline for Companies

  • October - End of December: Sign up for the program
  • January: Receive recommendations and resumes from HackDFW. Set up interviews for candidates.
  • February - March: Make offers to candidates

Timeline for Students

  • October - End of December: Apply to be a Fellow
  • January: HackDFW interviews candidates and extends recommendations to companies.
  • February - March: Get accepted to the Fellowship or continue to re-interview.

Benefits to You

  • You will have access to students from a variety of schools and backgrounds.
  • Applicants will be prescreened by HackDFW staff, so you won’t have to sift through candidates that don’t reach requirements, such as GPA. We provide a rigorous technical and behavioral screening process.
  • Recruitment will happen through hackathons and the hackathon community, so many applicants will be students who are passionate about software and hardware development.
  • Our reach will allow you to have access to applicants who originally may not have known of your company or opportunities.
  • HackDFW will provide group housing for the summer interns.
  • HackDFW will provide programming and weekly events for the entire fellowship group.
  • Your process is significantly reduced to providing requirements for interns, and following up on the HackDFW recommendations. In doing this, you will save up to six months' worth of time usually spent on recruitment.

Requirements for Companies

  • Interns must be paid a minimum of $22/hr.
  • HackDFW will send over several interns for interviews. One portion of this interview must be on-site with the company.
  • Companies must agree to pay HackDFW $5000 for transportation, housing, and programming expenses per intern.
  • Companies must agree to take a minimum of 2 interns.
